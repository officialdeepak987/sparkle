Frequently Asked Questions

Do you travel for outstation wedding shoots?

+

Yes, we are based in Noida but we love traveling across India and internationally to capture your special day. Travel and accommodation charges apply for outstation shoots.

How long does it take to deliver photos/videos?

+

We provide a raw data dump or a "sneak peek" selection within 48 hours. The complete edited gallery and cinematic films are typically delivered within 4 to 6 weeks.

What is your payment structure?

+

We require a 30% advance to block the dates, 50% on the day of the event, and the remaining 20% before the delivery of the final edited deliverables.
