Got Questions?

Frequently Asked
Questions

Do you travel for outstation weddings? +
Yes! We cover weddings across India and are available for destination shoots anywhere — from the hills of Uttarakhand to the beaches of Goa.
How far in advance should we book? +
We recommend booking at least 3–6 months in advance, especially for peak wedding season (October–February). Popular dates fill up fast!
What is included in your wedding packages? +
Our packages typically include pre-wedding consultation, full-day ceremony coverage, edited photos, cinematic highlight reel, online gallery access, and a premium photobook. Custom packages available.
How long does delivery take? +
Edited photos are delivered within 3–4 weeks. Final cinematic films are delivered within 6–8 weeks after the wedding date.
Do you offer drone photography? +
Absolutely! Our drone cinematography creates breathtaking aerial footage of your venue, baraat, and ceremonies. We carry all necessary permissions and equipment.
Can we see a full wedding album before booking? +
Of course! We're happy to share full wedding galleries during your consultation so you can see the quality and storytelling style across an entire event.
